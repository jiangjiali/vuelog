## 认识习惯
> 习惯分为三类：下意识的行为、下意识的想法、下意识做出的决定

> 影响习惯的大脑结构：基底核、突触

### 习惯影响基因：
* 日常习惯可能导致人体开启或关闭某种遗传基因，这种现象通常被称为“基因表达”或“表观遗传”。  
* 开启恶性基因的坏习惯。  
* 好习惯及其作用包括。

在《习惯的力量》一书中，作者查尔斯·杜希格通过出色的研究告诉我们，习惯执行的过程分为“暗示”“惯常行为”和“奖赏”三个阶段。“暗示”指的是某种熟悉的环境参照物，它会向基底核发信号：该执行习惯了。每个习惯都是在不断重复这一过程，每个习惯都包含一个暗示、一次惯常行为和一份奖赏。
        
什么触发了习惯？视觉、听觉、时间、压力、人际交往的对象、信念和情绪
        
### 掌握有效的策略：

## Knowing Habits
> Habits are divided into three categories: subconscious behavior, subconscious thoughts, and subconscious decisions

> Brain structure that affects habits: basal nucleus, synapse

### Habit affects genes:
* Daily habits may cause the human body to turn on or off a certain genetic gene. This phenomenon is often called "gene expression" or "epigenetic".
* The bad habit of turning on vicious genes.
* Good habits and their functions include.

In the book "The Power of Habits", the author Charles Duhigg told us through excellent research that the process of habit execution is divided into three stages: "hint", "usual behavior" and "reward". "Hint" refers to a familiar environmental reference, which will signal to the basal nucleus that it is time to execute the habit. Every habit is constantly repeating this process, and every habit contains a hint, a habitual behavior, and a reward.
        
What triggers the habit? Vision, hearing, time, pressure, objects of interpersonal communication, beliefs and emotions
